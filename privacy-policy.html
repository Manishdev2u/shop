🔒 Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 1, 2025

Website: https://manishdevtip.shop

At ManishDevTips, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Details: Name, email address, and any information you provide during checkout or contact.
  • Purchase Information: Details of digital products purchased, download history, and payment status.
  • Technical Data: Browser type, IP address, device type, and operating system.
  • Usage Behavior: Pages visited, clicks, time spent on the site, and referring websites.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Deliver your purchased products
  • Send important updates and order confirmations
  • Respond to customer support requests
  • Improve our services and website functionality
  • Prevent fraudulent activity and ensure data security

3. Payment and Security

We do not store your full payment details. All transactions are handled securely through trusted third-party providers like Razorpay, UPI, or PayPal. Payment data is encrypted and processed through these platforms only.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We only share your data with trusted partners when necessary to process payments or provide services (e.g., hosting, analytics).

5. Cookies and Analytics

We use cookies and tools like Google Analytics to understand how users interact with our website. You can disable cookies in your browser settings if you prefer.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Ask us to correct or delete your data
  • Withdraw consent to use your data at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at: support@manishdevtip.shop

7. Data Retention

We keep your data only as long as necessary to fulfill your order, comply with legal requirements, or improve our services.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here with a new “Effective Date.”

9. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact:

📧 Email: support@manishdevtip.shop
